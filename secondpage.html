The Middle Eastern
Conflict

Current and dividing

The Middle Eastern region has often been an area of great conflict: the populations are heavily divided and, as always, a lot of "Western" interests are involved. The Israeli-Palestinian situation has been around since the birth of the Israel state in 1948, but it became more public only recently because of the start of the genocide. Even in this case, the other countries started paying attention when some threats were made.
