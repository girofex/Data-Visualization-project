Methodology

This website was created via multiple tools:

  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Material UI baseline for the front-end
  • D3.js for the creation of the plots
  • Python notebook, together with Numpy, Pandas and Matplotlib libraries, for the preprocessing of the data

Plots

As requested, we implemented 10 plots:

  1. Connection map
  2. Line chart
  3. Choropleth
  4. Stacked bar chart
  5. Polar chart
  6. Timeline
  7. Bar chart
  8. Network
  9. Area chart
  10. Pictorial unit chart

Pre-processing

The majority of the data that we found was usable as a ready-to-use csv file, after some sort of preprocessing.
For example, we changed the date format into DD-MM-YY, as used in our country, Italy.

Limitations

Not all of the data that we needed was available in csv format or similar.

  • For the Connection Map in the index displaying the armed conflicts in the world, we had to manually create a csv file with data found in the respective sources, found in the following References section.
  • For the Timeline in the second page explaining the history of the Middle East, we summarized the content of the respective source in the following section.
  • For the Network in the third page displaying the hierarchy of the Medellín Cartel, we summarized the relationships described in the related source in the following section.

We also had some difficulty in finding data about the cartel, in particular about monetary information on where Pablo Escobar's income was directed towards.
Our first idea was, in fact, to display where his money were used, to visualize how his help towards the poor was still very much obscured by his investments in the drug business.
Whether the missed data is due to it being classified or to the help of his followers, this is not something we ought to know.

We also struggled to classify the armed conflict happening in the world: many of them are caused by events that happened in the previous century, therefore they have developed during the years, becoming protests, civil wars, simple internal struggles or interstate wars.
To be as clear and truthful as possible, we considered the more recent and relevant events for each country.
